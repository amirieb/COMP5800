Syllabus and Textbooks

The required reading will be from the following list as well as select journal articles and conference papers (see below) which are available online to UML students:

  • [NCM] Networks, Crowds, and Markets: Reasoning About a Highly Connected World
    David Easley and Jon Kleinberg

  • [MMD] Mining of Massive Datasets
    Jure Leskovec, Anand Rajaraman, Jeffrey D. Ullman

  • [SNA] Social Network Data Analytics
    Editor: Charu C. Aggarwal
